An emotional closing page

When a bond stops feeling mutual

This page carries truth, silence, effort, and acceptance — written like a final letter that needed to be said.

The Message

I trusted you.

Not casually… not normally. You became a part of my life, and slowly — without realising — the centre of it.

And maybe that is exactly where I went wrong.

While I was getting closer, I could feel you moving away.

I noticed the dryness. The distance. The silence.

The things I did — the edits, the website, the time — were never for a “thank you”.

They were my way of building something real.

But your replies kept saying the same thing: this bond never meant the same to you.

What I Felt

01

5 days of silence — holding back every urge to text.

02

Deep attachment — this was never casual for me.

03

One-sided effort — I was carrying everything.

04

Final clarity — one-sided bonds don’t survive.

Acceptance

You will never know how difficult these last few days were. Not texting. Not reaching out. Just holding back.

I saw the pattern clearly — I was trying, you were distant.

Even when I shared something real, your replies felt… empty.

Maybe I gave more than what you were meant to receive.

And yes — it will take time to detach, because what I felt was real.

Now finally, our chapter is closing.

Take care. And thank you for all the memories.
