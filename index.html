Ek emotional closing page

Jab ek rishta dheere dheere ek jaisa feel hona band ho jaata hai

Yeh sirf ek message nahi hai… yeh woh sab hai jo maine feel kiya, notice kiya, aur finally samajh liya.

The Message

Maine tumpe trust kiya tha.

Aise hi casually nahi… dheere dheere tum meri life ka ek important part ban gaye the. Itna ki bina realise kiye, tum meri daily life ka centre ban gaye.

Shayad wahi meri sabse badi galti thi.

Kyunki jab main tumhare paas aa raha tha, tabhi mujhe feel hone laga ki tum mujhse door ja rahe ho.

Tumhare replies chhote hone lage… interest kam hone laga… effort almost khatam ho gaya.

Jo bhi maine tumhare liye kiya — edits, website, time — woh kabhi “thank you” ke liye nahi tha.

Main bas sach mein kuch real build karna chahta tha humare beech.

Lekin tumhara behaviour baar baar ek hi baat bol raha tha — yeh rishta tumhare liye utna important nahi hai jitna mere liye tha.

What I Felt

01

5 din tak maine khud ko roka… baar baar mann hua tumhe text karne ka.

02

Main emotionally attach ho chuka tha… yeh mere liye kabhi casual nahi tha.

03

Har baar main hi start karta tha… main hi effort daal raha tha.

04

Tab samajh aaya — yeh sab sirf ek side se ho raha tha.

Acceptance

Yeh last kuch din mere liye bilkul easy nahi the. Na tumhe text karna, na baat karna… bas khud ko control karna.

Dheere dheere mujhe pattern samajh aane laga — main is connection ko bachane ki koshish kar raha tha, aur tum usse chhodne lage the.

Jab bhi maine kuch important share kiya, tumhare replies bas formal se lagte the… jaise farq hi nahi padta.

Tab finally samajh aaya — yeh kabhi mutual tha hi nahi.

Shayad maine utna de diya, jitna tum kabhi lena hi nahi chahte the.

Aur haan… time lagega mujhe is sab se nikalne mein, kyunki jo maine feel kiya… woh sach tha.

Ab finally, humara chapter yahin khatam hota hai.

Apna khayal rakhna.

Thank you… jitni bhi memories thi unke liye.

Signing off with this — Swayam Mishra
